BATTERY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM OF CELL
A Battery Management System is the unsung hero behind the seamless functioning and longevity of batteries in a vast array of applications. At its core, a BMS is a sophisticated electronic system that monitors and manages the health, performance, and safety of battery packs. Whether it's ensuring the reliable operation of a mission-critical device, optimizing the range of an electric vehicle, or safeguarding the stability of a grid-connected energy storage system, the BMS is the sentinel that stands guard over the heart of modern energy storage technology.
